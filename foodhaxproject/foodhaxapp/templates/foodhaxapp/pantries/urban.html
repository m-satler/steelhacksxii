Urban League of Greater Pittsburgh - Hunger Services

Servicing individuals or families who reside in Allegheny, Beaver, Butler, Greene, Lawrence, Washington and Westmoreland Counties

Directions:

Address

332 Fifth Avenue, Fourth Floor, Pittsburgh, PA 15222

Important Notices

  • Emergency Food Assistance (EFA): The EFA refers eligible family living in Allegheny County to a broad network of neighborhood food pantries and community feeding facilities. The emergency pantry is a once-a-year safety net.

  • Food for Early Development (FED): FED helps eligible families that do not receive WIC benefits. FED offers baby formula and soft foods.

Monday - Friday:

  • Baby formula and food

Notes

These services are provided to individuals or families who reside in Allegheny, Beaver, Butler, Greene, Lawrence, Washington and Westmoreland Counties.
